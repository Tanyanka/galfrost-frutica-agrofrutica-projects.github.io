Agro Frutica Byshkiv LLC is a Ukrainian agricultural company, which specializes in berry production. It was founded in 2005 in Byshkiv village (Zhovkva district, Lviv region). This area has been famous for its berries cultivation, in particular strawberries.

We grow strawberry, Alpine strawberry, raspberry, chokeberry and cereals with European approach to quality, food safety and traceability. Our fresh berries are delivered to Ukrainian local market and processing companies. We have facilities for cooling and packing of fresh berries immediately after picking. So we can guarantee the best quality even for long range deliveries.
Having a vast experience in berries and cereals production, we are ready to provide also agricultural consultancy for smaller local berry producers.
